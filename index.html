About us

NEW BUILDING MATERIAL FOR BANGLADESH MARKET

Foam Concrete has developed a new and innovative concept for production of Foam Concrete blocks to be used as building materials. The method is cheap and Environmental friendly and meets the needs of developing countries.

The concept is built on energy friendly production based on recycled and new polystyrene bubbles and cement.

In houses: walls, floors and ceilings can be made of Foam concrete which is a light weight material. The material can also be used as dividing walls and for insulation materials in floors and ceilings in large buildings. Foam concrete has multi-purpose usages. Some of the customers It holds a number of positive material properties with respect to, insulation, fire resistance, building physics, maintenance and more. The houses will be inexpensive, quick to set up, easily adjusted for different uses and different appearances. At present the demand is increasing in the Bangladeshi Market. are using Foam concrete as floor raiser, partition walls, Generator rooms, roof-top Insulation and low cost housing etc.
